not replace `docs/known-gaps-phase7.md`; it narrows the JIT-specific handoff
items so later addendum prompts can close them one by one.

## Entry Format

- `Gap ID`: stable Cranelift-addendum identifier.
- `Layer`: API, feature gating, ABI, harness, native execution, benchmark
  fixtures, reports, or safety.
- `Evidence/Test`: current file, command, or missing artifact proving the gap.
- `Risk`: correctness, safety, portability, or measurement issue.
- `Planned handoff`: prompt expected to close or refine the gap.
